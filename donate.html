At ReStitch, we believe that every piece of clothing has potential. Instead of letting your old clothes sit in the back of your closet or end up in a landfill, donate them to us! Your pre-loved garments will be upcycled into fresh, stylish pieces for someone else to enjoy. By donating, you’re helping to reduce waste, promote sustainable fashion, and give clothes a brand-new purpose.

How to Donate

  1. Gather Your Clothes – We accept gently worn clothing of all kinds—tops, bottoms, dresses, jackets, and more. Make sure your items are clean and in good condition for upcycling.
  2. Ship or Drop Off – You can send your donation by mail or drop it off at one of our designated locations.
  3. Watch the Transformation – Your donated clothes will be creatively upcycled into new, unique garments. These pieces will then be available for purchase, continuing their journey in someone else’s wardrobe.

Get Involved

Want to do more? Spread the word and encourage friends and family to donate too! Together, we can make sustainable fashion the future.
